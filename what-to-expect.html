CONSULTATION & EXAM
Day One: Understanding YOU

On your first visit, Dr. Ruane will have the opportunity to sit down with you to discuss your chief complaint, answer any questions or concerns, and talk to you about your health goals. He will then talk to you about your patient history that you fill out on the online portal.

We will discuss and elaborate on how your current complaint manifested, and furthermore, will determine if there are any other complaints that you have that may be related via the nervous system. Once Dr. Ruane has gathered all the required details of your complaint and history, he will then begin the exam portion of your visit.â€‹

X-RAYS

Once the examination is complete, you will be scheduled to take outsourced X-rays at The Center of Diagnostic Imaging- McKinney.

The X-rays are an essential and vital form of data to rule out fracture and other pathologies, as well as to perform a line drawing analysis to determine the best approach to treatment.

REPORT OF FINDING
Day Two: How to Best Heal YOU

On your next visit, Dr. Ruane will discuss how your chief complaint relates to your X-ray findings. Together, we will talk about a treatment plan that best suits the needs of your condition.
